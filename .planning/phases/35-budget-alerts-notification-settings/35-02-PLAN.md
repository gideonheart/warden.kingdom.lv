     - Pass `config.permissionCooldownMs` to `pollSession()` as a third parameter
   - Modify `pollSession(sessionName, agentId)` signature to `pollSession(sessionName, agentId, cooldownMs: number)`
   - Update the `deduplicator.recordAndCheck(sessionName, state)` call to `deduplicator.recordAndCheck(sessionName, state, cooldownMs)`

3. **Wire BudgetAlertPoller and notification routes into server** (`src/server/index.ts`):

   - Start poller: `budgetAlertPoller.startPolling()` (after `notificationPoller.startPolling()`)
   - Stop poller in `handleShutdown()`: `budgetAlertPoller.stopPolling()` (after `notificationPoller.stopPolling()`, before `await telegramBotService.stop()`)
